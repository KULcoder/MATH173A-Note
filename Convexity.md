# Convexity



## Convex Functions

$f: \Omega \to \mathbb R$ is convex if 

- $\forall x, y \in \Omega$
- $\forall \alpha \in [0, 1]$

We have
$$
f(\alpha x + (1-\alpha) y ) \le \alpha f(x) + (1-\alpha)f(y)
$$
